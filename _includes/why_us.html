When you build with Majestic Homes you are creating a long lasting partnership working directly with us to build your dream home. In relationships with other builders home owners feel there is a fence between themselves and the builder, at Majestic Homes there is no fence! We only build 6-7 homes a year and the builder/owner of the company, Raz, is personally involved with each build. You have his email address and cell phone number.

Phase 1: Your Home, Your Design

Majestic Homes is a custom home builder, you work with a home designer to plan your dream home. Unlike other builders where you are limited to a small set of floor plans and elevations.

Some clients bring a very clear idea of what they want, while others start with a blank piece of paper and describe how they live and a plan materializes. Others browse through other floor plans and pick favourite elements from them.

Once you have a completed plan a starting price is provided and your agreement is signed with Majestic Homes that includes the closing date for your home.

Phase 2: Construction of Home, Flexibility and Partnership

Majestic Homes embraces the custom home process. Every option is flexible and under your control, unlike a standard builder that only offers limited selections for options. You visit local vendors specializing in each facet of your home. Here are just some of the options you will have control over during construction:

Exterior

  • Front Entrance Door
  • Window Size, Style and Color
  • Stone and Brick
  • Shingles and Fascia
  • Garage Door and Hardware

Interior

  • Cabinetry and Countertops
  • Door Style and Hardware
  • Hardwood, Carpet and Tiles
  • Baseboards and Trim
  • Paint and Stain Color

 

  • Lighting Fixtures
  • Plumbing Fixtures
  • Furnace
  • Fireplace
  • Electrical Outlets and Switches

The sheer number of selections can feel overwhelming at times but Majestic Homes will provide you with a framework of timelines for each selection and contact information for the vendors. As selections are made any additional pricing for options that are beyond standards will be provided. If you have any trouble making decisions Majestic Homes is there to help with ideas and discussion.

Phase 3: Possession of your Home, Warranty Coverage

The partnership with Majestic Homes doesn't end when you move into your home. Once settled you will have many opportunities to address any concerns with your home directly with Majestic Homes and through the Tarion New Home Warranty.

We want your home to be perfect and exactly as you imagined, if something isn't right we don't rest until you're satisfied!
