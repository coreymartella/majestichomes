Communities: Innerkip Greens

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy the peace and quiet of small town living

Innerkip Greens, located in the quaint village of Innerkip, is the latest new home release from Majestic Homes! Steps from the Thames River, 5 minutes from Woodstock, and less than 2 km from the Innerkip Highlands Golf Course.

Innerkip Greens is being built in two phases. Phase 1 is launched in Spring 2017. When Phase 2 is completed, this residential project will house 32 units comprising a mix of bungalows and 2-storey properties.
